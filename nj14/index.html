What's this all about?

Learn everything about the upcoming Nooku Framework 2.0 release with a spark of creativity, a sprinkle of magic and a lot of Nooku.

Expect two days of intense presentations, discussions, brainstorms, workshops, and hackathons around Nooku. Hosted at the uber awesome Creativity Lab of Flanders DC.

What is a Nooku Jam?

Nooku Jam is a two-day unconference event focussed mostly on developing with the Nooku. Jams are born from the desire for people to share and learn in an open environment.

It is an intense event full of discussions, hacking and interaction with developers, who are the main actors of the event.

It is a chance to network, learn something new and communicate what you're most passionate about.
